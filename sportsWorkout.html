Sport Workouts

Sports are the culmination of many exercises. In sports, you can have fun while exercising or doing physical activity.



These are some sports that are practiced by the website owner that they highly recommend!
Arnis/Kali
Arnis, the national sports of the Philippines is a fun combat sport that tests, your strength and endurance. It also develops your reaction time and tests it against real time opponents when sparring.
Badminton
Using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net, you can test your endurance, reaction time, cardio, and your ability control your strength when hitting.
Cycling
Using a bicycle you can trek long distances and feel the cool breeze as you cycle through the area. This will test your cardio and leg endurance, and can help develop those types of fitness.
